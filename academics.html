KNOWLEDGE · DISCIPLINE · VALUES

Academics

A CBSE-aligned, student-centered approach that prepares learners for academic excellence and lifelong success.

A Strong Foundation for Lifelong Learning

At Christ Mission School, academics are designed to provide students with a strong educational foundation that combines knowledge, discipline, creativity, and values. As a CBSE-oriented institution, we follow a structured and student-centered approach that prepares learners for academic excellence and lifelong success.

Our academic system focuses on conceptual understanding, critical thinking, communication skills, and holistic development, ensuring that every child receives quality education in a supportive environment.

CBSE CURRICULUM

A Balanced, Nationally Recognized Framework

Christ Mission School follows the CBSE-based academic framework, designed to provide balanced and nationally recognized education. Our curriculum encourages:

  • Concept-based learning
  • Analytical thinking
  • Practical understanding
  • Skill development
  • Continuous improvement

We aim to create confident learners who are prepared for higher education and future opportunities.

Academic Session

April to March, aligned with the Indian financial year pattern.

Two Terms

First Term & Mid-Term Assessments → Second Term & Final Examinations.

Continuous Assessment

Regular class tests, assignments, projects, and activities throughout the year.

OUR CAMPUS & FACILITIES

Lush Green Environment & Play Fields

A beautiful, tree-filled campus in Mirganj, Purnea, designed to provide a fresh, eco-friendly, and inspiring environment for academic and physical development.

Lush Green Campus

Sprawling lawns surrounded by fruit-bearing trees, offering a serene, fresh, and beautiful environment where students can learn and grow in nature's lap.

Cricket Nets

Dedicated batting pitch equipped with professional safety netting, designed to help students refine their batting and bowling techniques.

Football Fields

Spacious sports fields with professional goals, providing ample room for matches, team building, and athletic sportsmanship.

Scenic Playgrounds

Beautifully manicured athletic grounds that foster fitness, discipline, and a healthy lifestyle among students of all age groups.

Nature & Ecology

An organic, orchard-like setting where students gain hands-on ecological awareness amid natural local flora and fruit orchards.
